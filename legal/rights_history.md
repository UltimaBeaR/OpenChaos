# Urban Chaos Rights History

Timeline of ownership of rights to the game Urban Chaos (1999), based on publicly confirmed facts.
All statements are backed by source links.


## Timeline

### 1997 — Founding of MuckyFoot Productions Ltd

Three former Bullfrog Productions developers founded the company **Mucky Foot Productions Limited**
in Guildford, UK:

- **Mike Diskett** (lead programmer of Urban Chaos, later the author of the source code release)
- **Fin McGechie**
- **Guy Simmons** (implemented the editor's windowing system, later the current rights holder)
- **Gary Carr** joined as the 4th director shortly after the founding

Sources:
- [Mucky Foot Productions — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mucky_Foot_Productions)
- [Mucky Foot Productions — Guildford Games](https://guildford.games/developers/mucky-foot)
- [original_game/README.md](../original_game/README.md) — author's description by Mike Diskett

### 1999 — Release of Urban Chaos

- **Developer:** Mucky Foot Productions Ltd
- **Publisher:** Eidos Interactive
- **Platforms:** PC (Windows), PlayStation, Dreamcast
- **Publishing terms:** Eidos received a **license to publish on specific platforms**.
  The IP (intellectual property), code, and trademark remained with MuckyFoot.

Confirmation from Mike Diskett (handle "disky") on the GOG.com forum:

> *"MF retained ownership of the IP and code (including trademark), Eidos had a license
> to publish on specific platforms"*

Sources:
- [GOG.com Forum — discussion of the source code release](https://www.gog.com/forum/general/muckyfoots_urban_chaos_source_code_released_on_github)

### November 2003 — Closure of MuckyFoot

The company closed after financial difficulties and entered liquidation proceedings.

Sources:
- [Mucky Foot Closes Doors — Game Developer](https://www.gamedeveloper.com/game-platforms/mucky-foot-closes-doors)

### 2003–2004 — Directors bought back the IP from the liquidators

Former MuckyFoot directors (including Mike Diskett and Guy Simmons) bought back the company's
intellectual property from the liquidators.

Confirmation from Mike Diskett on GOG.com:

> *"The MF directors purchased the MF IP's from the liquidators"*

Sources:
- [GOG.com Forum — discussion of the source code release](https://www.gog.com/forum/general/muckyfoots_urban_chaos_source_code_released_on_github)

### January 2004 — Registration of My Little Planet Ltd

Guy Simmons registered the company **My Little Planet Limited** (Companies House UK, #05028317).

- Registration date: 28 January 2004
- Status: Active
- Sole current director: **SIMMONS, Guy Rolfe**

The company was initially used for consulting; later the rights to the MuckyFoot games
were re-registered through it.

Sources:
- [My Little Planet Ltd — Companies House UK](https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/company/05028317)
- [My Little Planet Ltd — Companies House Officers](https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/company/05028317/officers)

### 2009 — Square Enix absorbed Eidos

Square Enix acquired SCi Entertainment Group (the parent company of Eidos Interactive).
Along with Eidos, Square Enix obtained the **license to publish** Urban Chaos (but not the IP).

Sources:
- [Eidos Interactive — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eidos_Interactive)

### 2012–2013 — Urban Chaos appeared on Steam and GOG

Square Enix published Urban Chaos on digital storefronts, using the license inherited
from Eidos.

Sources:
- [Urban Chaos — PCGamingWiki](https://www.pcgamingwiki.com/wiki/Urban_Chaos)
- [Urban Chaos \[RELISTED\] — Delisted Games](https://delistedgames.com/urban-chaos/)

### 20 May 2017 — Mike Diskett published the source code

Mike Diskett (GitHub: [dizzy2003](https://github.com/dizzy2003)) published the Urban Chaos source code
under the **MIT license** on GitHub.

- Repository: [dizzy2003/MuckyFoot-UrbanChaos](https://github.com/dizzy2003/MuckyFoot-UrbanChaos)
- License: MIT (see LICENSE)
- Copyright in the license: `Copyright (c) 2017 dizzy2003` (original code)
- Game assets **not included** — source code only
- At the time of writing: 250 stars, 49 forks, no DMCA requests

Quote from the README:
> *"I am making this code public mainly out of historical interest"*

Sources:
- [GitHub — MuckyFoot-UrbanChaos](https://github.com/dizzy2003/MuckyFoot-UrbanChaos)
- [Internet Archive — source code archive](https://archive.org/details/urban-chaos-src)

### 2 October 2019 — Delisting from Steam and GOG

Urban Chaos was **removed** from both storefronts. Reason: expiration of licensing rights.

Statement from GOG.com:
> *"Due to expiring licensing rights, Urban Chaos will be delisted from our catalog today"*

This fact **directly confirms** that Square Enix did not own the IP — it only had a
license to publish, which had an expiration date.

Sources:
- [Urban Chaos \[RELISTED\] — Delisted Games](https://delistedgames.com/urban-chaos/)

### 8 August 2020 — Relisting on Steam

Urban Chaos **returned** to Steam. New publisher and rights holder: **My Little Planet Ltd**.

Sources:
- [Urban Chaos — Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/243060/Urban_Chaos/)
- [Urban Chaos \[RELISTED\] — Delisted Games](https://delistedgames.com/urban-chaos/)

### 19 January 2021 — Relisting on GOG.com

Sources:
- [Spillhistorie.no — Startopia og Urban Chaos er tilbake på GOG](https://spillhistorie.no/2021/01/20/startopia-og-urban-chaos-er-tilbake-pa-gog/)

### May 2022 — Embracer bought Eidos

Embracer Group acquired Crystal Dynamics, Eidos-Montréal, Square Enix Montréal, and the IP
catalog (including Tomb Raider, Deus Ex, Thief, Legacy of Kain, and 50+ games) for $300M.

**Urban Chaos was NOT part** of this deal — the IP already belonged to My Little Planet Ltd,
not to Square Enix/Eidos.

Sources:
- [Embracer Group — press release](https://embracer.com/releases/embracer-group-enters-into-an-agreement-to-acquire-eidos-crystal-dynamics-and-square-enix-montreal-amongst-other-assets/)

### 13 October 2025 — Third-party mods on Steam

A paid mod [Maddie Modder Mods for Urban Chaos](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3992700/Maddie_Modder_Mods_for_Urban_Chaos/)
was published on Steam by a third-party developer (morus.pro / maddic.club), created using the open
source code. The mod is sold freely on the storefront, right next to the game itself.


## Current Rights Ownership (summary)

| What | Owner | Confirmation |
|-----|----------|---------------|
| Game IP (intellectual property) | **My Little Planet Ltd** (Guy Simmons) | Steam listing, delisting/relisting 2019–2020 |
| Trademark "Urban Chaos" | **My Little Planet Ltd** (Guy Simmons) | Mike Diskett quote: "MF retained... trademark" |
| Source code | Public MIT license from Mike Diskett | [GitHub](https://github.com/dizzy2003/MuckyFoot-UrbanChaos), LICENSE |
| Game assets (textures, sounds, levels, video) | **My Little Planet Ltd** (Guy Simmons) | Not included in the MIT release |
| Former Eidos / Square Enix / Embracer rights | **None** — license expired 2019 | GOG/Steam delisting |
